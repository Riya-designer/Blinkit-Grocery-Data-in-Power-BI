# Blinkit-Grocery-Data-in-Power-BI
# 📊 Blinkit Sales Analysis – Data Storytelling

## 🟡 1. Business Context (Opening Story)

Blinkit, a fast-growing quick-commerce platform, aims to optimize its **sales performance, customer satisfaction, and inventory distribution**.

The goal of this analysis is to answer:

* What drives sales?
* Which products perform best?
* How do outlet characteristics impact revenue?
* Where can Blinkit improve?

---

## 🎯 2. Key KPIs (What We Measured)

To evaluate performance, we focused on:

* 💰 **Total Sales** → Overall revenue performance
* 📈 **Average Sales** → Revenue per transaction
* 📦 **Number of Items Sold** → Demand volume
* ⭐ **Average Rating** → Customer satisfaction

---

## 📌 3. Insight 1: Sales by Fat Content

### 🧠 Story:

When analyzing product categories based on fat content (Low Fat vs Regular):

* **Regular fat products generated higher total sales**
* However, **low-fat products had better average ratings**

### 💡 Insight:

Customers are **buying taste-driven products more**, but **health-conscious products drive satisfaction**

👉 **Business Opportunity:**

* Promote low-fat items better
* Bundle healthy products with popular items

---

## 📌 4. Insight 2: Sales by Item Type

### 🧠 Story:

Different product categories contribute unevenly to revenue:

* **Top-performing categories**: Snacks, Dairy, Beverages
* **Low-performing categories**: Some niche or specialty items

### 💡 Insight:

High-frequency consumption items dominate revenue.

👉 **Business Opportunity:**

* Focus inventory & promotions on high-demand categories
* Re-evaluate or reposition low-performing items

---

## 📌 5. Insight 3: Fat Content by Outlet

### 🧠 Story:

Across different outlets:

* Some outlets sell more **regular items**
* Others perform better in **low-fat segments**

### 💡 Insight:

Customer preferences vary by location/outlet.

👉 **Business Opportunity:**

* Personalize inventory per outlet
* Use regional demand patterns for stocking

---

## 📌 6. Insight 4: Sales by Outlet Establishment

### 🧠 Story:

Older vs newer outlets show different performance:

* **Established outlets generate higher sales**
* New outlets are still building demand

### 💡 Insight:

Time plays a key role in customer trust and repeat purchases.

👉 **Business Opportunity:**

* Run promotions for new outlets
* Improve visibility & delivery speed

---

## 📌 7. Insight 5: Sales by Outlet Size

### 🧠 Story:

* **Medium & Large outlets contribute the highest sales**
* Small outlets have limited inventory → lower sales

### 💡 Insight:

Bigger outlets = more variety = more sales

👉 **Business Opportunity:**

* Expand high-performing outlet sizes
* Optimize inventory in smaller outlets

---

## 📌 8. Insight 6: Sales by Location

### 🧠 Story:

* Urban areas dominate revenue
* Some regions underperform

### 💡 Insight:

Demand is highly location-driven.

👉 **Business Opportunity:**

* Focus marketing in high-performing cities
* Improve logistics in low-performing areas

---

## 📌 9. Insight 7: Metrics by Outlet Type

### 🧠 Story:

Different outlet types (Grocery Store, Supermarket, etc.) show:

* Variation in **sales, ratings, and item count**
* Some outlets balance both **sales + satisfaction**
* Others focus only on volume

### 💡 Insight:

Not all outlets are optimized equally.

👉 **Business Opportunity:**

* Benchmark best-performing outlet types
* Standardize successful strategies

---

# 🧩 10. Final Conclusion (Closing Story)

From this analysis, we discovered:

✔ Sales are driven by **high-demand categories and outlet size**
✔ Customer satisfaction is higher in **health-focused products**
✔ Performance varies significantly by **location and outlet type**

---

## 🚀 Final Recommendations:

* Improve **product mix (health vs taste balance)**
* Personalize **inventory per outlet/location**
* Strengthen **new outlet growth strategy**
* Focus on **top-performing categories for revenue boost**
